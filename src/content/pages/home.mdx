Hi to all you lovely people, side-anchored minimalist theme designed for developers, writers, and digital gardeners.
Built on [Astro]. Nothing in the way, just your words on a clean, fast canvas.

This site is the live demo and documentation for Sidey.
Head over to [Getting Started] to set up your own.

## Journey

- **2023**:
  Company had survived. At some point, two happy accidents heppened to me: first is I asked my partner to invest in new version of our product, He helped us. Second is in the light of candles on day with black out after russian attacking kyiv we signed our first US customer.

- **2022**:
  The war arrived in Kyiv. First of all, I believe that the world we experienced over the last few decades before 2022 was probably the most stable and predictable period we will see in our lifetime. Our business is dying.

- **2019**:
  Against all odds idea had survived. My first company Finceh LLC secured first account. It was Bank Clearing House (now Bank.KD). Then we lauched 25+ companies in Ukraine who was working on our lending uromation platform.

- **2018**:
  I left the microfinance company Express Finance. It was a great time in the role of CIO. I met my future partners and developers there. I proposed to upper management that we open a software company to sell a lending automation platform to financial companies and banks, but I was informed that this idea had no chance of surviving.

- **2015**:
  I joined Bank Mykhailivskyi. I climbed the career ladder from a call center operator to an IT Project Manager for the call center. It was my first IT role, and I used the chance to develop my career in the IT industry.

- **2014**:
  When Russia occupied Luhansk, my family and I had to move to Kyiv to start life from scratch. It's not the best feeling to be a refugee in your own country. 

- **2011**:
  I became the happy father of an amazing baby girl. I'm still figuring out the role, but she is the best 'project' I've ever worked on.

- **2010**:
  I joined [Privatbank], After a couple of shots of vodka at some point in 2010, I was invited to an interview to work as a financial specialist at a bank branch. The interview went well, and I accepted my first job.

- **2005**:
  I started at the East Ukrainian National University of Volodymyr Dahl. I studied engineering because I was dreaming of a career in the automotive industry.

- **1988**:
  On the 28th of June, I was born in Luhansk. It's in the eastern part of Ukraine, which was later occupied by Russia in 2014
 

## Tech stack & Deployment

It's a static site built with [Astro] and [Tailwind CSS]. Deploy anywhere that supports static site hosting, such as [Cloudflare Pages], [GitHub Pages], or [Netlify].
